No. The calculator gives a rough estimate only. Hakim will confirm the latest figure based on your loan amount, bank rate, tenure, down payment, and approval profile.

What is included in the OTR price? add

Hakim can explain the latest on-the-road price, insurance estimate, registration, current campaign, and branch offer clearly before you decide.

How do I know which Honda model or variant fits me? add

Share your budget, family size, driving style, and preferred body type. Hakim can narrow the options and compare the most suitable models or variants for you.

Is there ready stock, or do I need to wait? add

Stock and waiting time can change by model, colour, variant, and branch allocation. Hakim will check the latest availability before you proceed.

What documents do I need for loan checking? add

Hakim will guide you based on your situation. Usually buyers prepare IC, driving licence, income documents, bank statements, or company documents if self-employed.

Can I trade in my current car? add

Yes. Send your car model, year, mileage, condition, and photos. Hakim can advise the next step and help you estimate whether trade-in makes sense.

Can I book a test drive before deciding? add

Yes. Tell Hakim the model you want to try and your preferred area or date. He will help check the available slot.

Do promos change every month? add

Yes, campaigns and branch offers may change. It is better to confirm the latest promo, rebate, stock, and estimated delivery timing directly with Hakim.
